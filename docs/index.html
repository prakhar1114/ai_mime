AI Mime

Record, refine, and replay workflows on macOS.

AI Mime is a native macOS RPA tool designed to empower computer-use agents with the context they need to succeed. It moves beyond simple text prompts by capturing rich ground truth, refining it into parameterized instructions, and replaying workflows on demand.

Why AI Mime?

Computer-use models are getting better, but RPA still breaks down when instructions are low-context and tasks run for a long time. AI Mime makes automation reliable by grounding agents in real demonstrations.

Rich context, not vague prompts

Record what actually happened—UI state, clicks, keystrokes—so the agent isn’t guessing.

Strict, observable boundaries

Refine recordings into parameterized subtasks with clear inputs and dependencies.

Less “creativity”, more repeatability

Replay workflows with high fidelity across native apps and the web—on demand.

Production-shaped workflows

Turn a one-off trace into something reusable, inspectable, and easy to iterate on.
